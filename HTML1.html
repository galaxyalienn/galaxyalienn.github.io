The Moon Jelly

Lifestyle of the Aurelia Aurita

Friendly Floaties

The Aurelia Aurita, or Moon Jelly, is one of the most friendly species of jellyfish and is most recognizable by:

  • The Unique Design in its membraine
  • Warm Tropical ocean geography
  • Generaally one of the smaller species of Jellies

Reproduction

The Jellyfish reproduce similar to other wildlife, but an intresting effect happens afterward. During the process in which the female Jellies release the polyps, these polyps are actually asexual and can grow buds that produce more polyps. So not only do the moon jellies reproduce sexualy, but the polyps after release grow asexual as well, giving them a high reproduction rate.
